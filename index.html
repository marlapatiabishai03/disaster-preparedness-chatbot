ℹ️ About This Project

An educational disaster management system for college students

The Disaster Preparedness and Response Education System is a community-focused platform designed to improve awareness and readiness for natural disasters.

It provides structured information on different types of disasters and educates users on the correct actions to take before, during, and after an emergency.

The system includes an interactive, rule-based chatbot that guides users with disaster safety instructions, laying the foundation for future intelligent and real-time features.
